I was mainly responsible for topic conception, AutoML and Deep Learning in this project.

Drawing on the experience of our last project, we first clarified the research direction and research subjects, set expected outcomes based on our research objects, and advanced our research purposefully towards our goals. Throughout the process, I applied deep learning and AutoML in practical research for the first time. I developed a deeper understanding of models including CNN, LSTM and Transformer, and built a more systematic awareness of environmental data processing.

I am truly grateful to all team members for their full commitment to the project, which enabled us to produce plenty of meaningful research findings.
